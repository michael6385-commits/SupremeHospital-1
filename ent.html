Advanced Diagnostics & Surgery

Ear and nose conditions often include infections, allergies, and structural issues like sinusitis, tinnitus, and deviated septum. Proper diagnosis by an ENT specialist is essential for effective management.

Depending on the clinical diagnosis, treatment options may range from medical management (prescribing medication or recommended lifestyle changes) to advanced surgical procedures.
