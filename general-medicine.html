Frequently Asked Questions

You should consult a general physician for routine health check-ups, persistent symptoms like prolonged fever or unexplained pain, management of chronic conditions, or when experiencing unusual health changes. They provide comprehensive care and refer to specialists if necessary.

General physicians play a crucial role by providing continuous care, monitoring health status, adjusting treatments, and coordinating with specialists. They educate patients on lifestyle modifications to prevent complications.

Look for a physician with good experience, patient-friendly communication, and accessibility. Choose someone who listens, explains clearly, and understands your medical history.

Bring a list of your symptoms, past medical history, medications, and any previous test results. Be ready to discuss your lifestyle, habits, and concerns honestly.
