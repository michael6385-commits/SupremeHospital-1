Frequently Asked Questions

Psychiatric medications are safe when managed by a qualified professional. Whether you need them long-term depends on your specific diagnosis and clinical progress.

The duration varies based on the condition being treated; some may find relief in months, while others benefit from ongoing clinical support.

You can expect a thorough evaluation of your mental and physical health history, discussion of symptoms, and development of an initial treatment plan.

If your mental health is significantly impacting your daily life, relationships, or overall well-being, consulting a psychiatrist can help you find relief.
