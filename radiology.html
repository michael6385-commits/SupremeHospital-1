Advanced Technology

Imaging Modalities

Supreme Radiology offers a comprehensive range of imaging modalities, each meticulously selected to serve specific diagnostic purposes.

X-ray

Fast and reliable imaging for evaluating bones, chest, and basic diagnostic needs.

CT or CAT scan

Advanced cross-sectional imaging delivering high-resolution, accurate, and rapid diagnosis.

MRI

Detailed imaging of soft tissues, brain, spine, joints, and internal organs without radiation.

Ultrasound

Real-time imaging using sound waves for abdominal, pelvic, cardiac, and pregnancy assessments.

Mammography

Specialized breast imaging for early detection, screening, and accurate diagnosis.

Interventional Radiology

Minimally invasive, image-guided procedures for targeted diagnosis and treatment.
